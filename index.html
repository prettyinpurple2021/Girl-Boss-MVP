Brand Values Questionnaire

1. Your Guiding Stars: What are the three most important principles that light your business path?

2. World Changer: What positive impact do you dream of making on your customers and the planet?

3. Problem Solver: What challenges are you tackling for your customers?

4. Your Secret Sauce: What makes your business sparkle and shine compared to the competition?

5. Superpowers: What are your business's greatest strengths?

6. Brand Vibes: If your company was a person, what would their personality be like?

7. Your North Star: What's your big, bold vision for the future of your business?

8. Goal Getter: What are the top goals you're determined to achieve?
